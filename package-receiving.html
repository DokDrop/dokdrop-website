PACKAGE RECEIVING

Your boat finally has an address.

Marinas accept packages reluctantly: some won't sign, most have nowhere secure to hold them, and every parcel is handled differently. Crews end up timing their day around catching a driver on the dock. DokDrop replaces all of it.

The Process

How it works

Step 1

Use our address at any checkout.

Amazon, chandleries, courier shipments, personal orders: address them to your vessel, care of DokDrop.

Step 2

We sign and hold.

Every parcel is signed for, logged with a photo, and stored securely, refrigerated or climate-controlled when the contents call for it.

Step 3

We run it to the boat.

On your standing schedule, or on demand when something urgent lands. Dockside or by water to anchor.

FAQ

Questions crews ask.

Can suppliers deliver to you directly?

Yes. Any courier, freight forwarder, or local supplier can drop at our receiving address during desk hours.

What about oversized or heavy items?

We handle everything a runner and a hand truck can move; for freight-scale items, contact dispatch first and we'll arrange it.

Do you deliver to anchor?

Yes. Water relay to Key Biscayne and Biscayne Bay anchorages is part of the service.
