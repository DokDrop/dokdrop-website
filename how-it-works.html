THE PROCESS · I

From request to stowed aboard.

One concierge coordinates the entire order — sourcing, quality-checking, and delivering to your vessel at dock or anchor. Here's exactly how it runs.

Step 1

Submit your request

Tell us the vessel, marina/slip or anchor coordinates, and delivery window. Order by category, itemized list, or a quick call to the concierge desk.

Step 2 · Usually within 15 min

Confirmation & quote

Your concierge confirms pricing and availability, flags any substitutions, and locks the delivery window.

Step 3

Sourcing begins

We fan out to vetted purveyors — butchers, produce markets, chandleries, wine & spirits — sourcing everything against your list.

Step 4

Quality check & staging

Every item is checked on arrival at our supply dock. Perishables are iced and packed for transit before departure.

Step 5

Delivered dockside or to anchor

Brought aboard by dock cart or tender, timed to your schedule and handed directly to crew — discreet, on time, no disruption to guest prep.

Step 6

Confirmation & receipt

A photo of everything stowed aboard and an itemized receipt land in your inbox the moment delivery is complete.

Questions

Frequently asked.

Do you deliver to anchor, not just marinas?

Yes. Most orders go dockside, but if you're anchored in Biscayne Bay or nearby, we coordinate tender delivery directly to the vessel.

What if I need something last-minute, day-of?

Our concierge desk runs rush requests every charter day. Mark your order urgent and we'll confirm availability immediately — most in-stock rush orders arrive in under 90 minutes.

How is pricing handled — do you mark up items?

You're quoted purveyor cost plus a transparent concierge and delivery fee before we source anything. No surprises on the receipt.

Can I set up recurring orders for back-to-back charters?

Yes — standing orders can be configured once per season and adjusted per charter, so restocking between guests is automatic.

What areas of Miami do you cover?

Miami Beach Marina, Sunset Harbour, Island Gardens, Monty's, Key Biscayne, Fisher Island, and anchorages across Biscayne Bay. Ask if your location isn't listed.

Do you handle alcohol or items requiring ID/licensing?

Yes, through our licensed purveyor partners. An adult crew member must be present to sign for delivery.
