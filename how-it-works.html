THE PROCESS

From request to stowed aboard.

Step 1

Submit your request

Tell us the vessel, marina/slip or anchor coordinates, delivery window, and where to pick up from: your usual supplier, or one we already work with.

Step 2 · Usually within 15 min

Confirmed & dispatched

We confirm your delivery fee and lock the pickup window. You pay the supplier directly for the goods themselves, at the same price as walking in.

Step 3

Runner heads to pickup

Your runner heads straight to the market, chandlery, or liquor store you named and collects the order, already paid for or on your account.

Step 4

Order confirmed complete

A quick check against your list before the runner leaves the supplier, so nothing's missing when it reaches the boat.

Step 5

Delivered dockside or to anchor

Brought aboard by dock cart or tender, timed to your schedule and handed directly to crew.

Step 6

Confirmation & receipt

A photo of the handoff and your delivery receipt land in your inbox the moment it's aboard.

Questions

Frequently asked.

Do you deliver to anchor, not just marinas?

Yes. Most orders go dockside, but if you're anchored in Biscayne Bay or nearby, we coordinate tender delivery directly to the vessel.

What if I need something last-minute, day-of?

Our runner network handles rush requests every day. Mark your order urgent and we'll dispatch the nearest available runner immediately.

How is pricing handled, and do you mark up items?

We never touch the price of the goods themselves. You pay your supplier directly, the same as walking in. DokDrop only charges a flat delivery fee on top, or nothing extra if you're on a membership.

Can I set up recurring orders for back-to-back charters?

Yes. A DokDrop membership includes a set number of deliveries each month at no extra charge, with a discount on anything beyond that. Set it up once; it renews automatically.

Do you sell or stock the groceries and supplies yourselves?

No. DokDrop is the delivery layer, not the store. You order from the supplier you already trust, and a runner handles pickup and delivery. That means you're always paying retail price for the goods themselves.

What areas of Miami do you cover?

Miami Beach Marina, Sunset Harbour, Island Gardens, Monty's, Key Biscayne, Fisher Island, and anchorages across Biscayne Bay. Ask if your location isn't listed.
