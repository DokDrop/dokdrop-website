ABOUT DOKDROP · III

Built for the hour before guests board.

Charter provisioning has always meant crew running dockside errands against the clock. DokDrop exists to take that off your list entirely.

Why Concierge

One point of contact. Every purveyor already vetted.

DokDrop was built around a simple observation: the best charter crews in Miami were spending hours a week driving to markets, chandleries, and liquor stores instead of preparing the vessel.

We replaced that with a single concierge relationship. You send one request; we run it through a network of purveyors we already trust — the same ones, order after order — so quality stays consistent and nothing falls through on delivery day.

What We Value

How we operate on the water.

Discretion

Deliveries are quiet and timed around guests — crew coordinate the handoff, never the owner.

Reliability

Confirmed windows are held. If something can't be sourced in time, you hear it early — not at the dock.

Local Relationships

Every purveyor in our network is one we've worked with directly — not a marketplace of unknowns.

Care for the Vessel

Every crew member handling a delivery is briefed on boarding etiquette, stowage, and handling aboard.
