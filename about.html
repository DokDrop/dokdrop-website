ABOUT DOKDROP

For the boats too busy to make the run themselves.

Big charter provisioners are built for week-long trips with a chef and a budget. Delivery apps stop at the marina gate. DokDrop covers what's in between — someone to make the run when your crew doesn't have time to.

Why DokDrop

You already know your suppliers. We just make the run.

Crews on mid-size boats and day charters lose hours to the same dockside errands — and no provisioning company is built for a trip that starts this afternoon, not next month.

We don't stock inventory or take a cut. You order from the supplier you'd already choose — your usual market, your usual chandlery — and a runner brings it aboard. Pay the supplier their normal price; DokDrop just charges for the run.

What We Value

How we operate on the water.

Discretion

Deliveries are quiet and timed around guests — a runner coordinates the handoff with crew, never the owner.

Reliability

Confirmed windows are held. If a supplier can't fill your order in time, you hear it early — not at the dock.

Local Suppliers

You order from real Miami businesses — the market, chandlery, or shop already down the street from your marina.

Care for the Vessel

Every runner is briefed on boarding etiquette and dockside handoff before their first delivery.
